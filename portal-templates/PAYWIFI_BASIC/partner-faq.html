Partner FAQ

Common questions about being a PAYWIFI partner.

How much do I earn per transaction?

You keep 30% of every paid transaction routed to your store. The remaining 70% is owed to PAYWIFI as remittance. Both percentages are fixed by platform policy.

How often do I need to remit?

Weekly. Each Monday at 23:59 Asia/Manila is the deadline for remitting 70% of the previous week's gross sales.

What happens if I miss the weekly deadline?

The system automatically flips your account to restricted. You can still sign in and submit a remittance, but you cannot confirm new payments and your store is removed from the customer dropdown. The system reinstates you automatically once your outstanding balance is cleared.

How do I remit?

Open the Remit tab. The recommended method is GCash — send the amount you owe to PAYWIFI's GCash number, then submit the reference number with method "gcash" for admin approval. Bank transfer and cash are also accepted.

What is the customer flow?
  1. Customer connects to PAYWIFI Wi-Fi.
  2. They pick a plan and choose your store at checkout.
  3. They pay you cash in person.
  4. You confirm the payment in your dashboard.
  5. We generate the voucher and SMS it to the customer.
How do I confirm a payment?

From your dashboard, tap Confirm on the row in Awaiting confirmation. Tick "I have received the cash" and tap the green button. The voucher is generated immediately and SMS-sent to the customer's phone (if they provided one).

What if the customer didn't pay or paid the wrong amount?

Use the Decline button. Pick a reason: No-show, Wrong amount, Suspicious, or Other. The decline is logged for admin review and the customer's payment stays in their portal as cancelled.

How long do I have to confirm a payment?

Target: 5 minutes. If a payment sits longer than that, we'll SMS you a reminder. Repeated late confirmations may trigger admin review.

I forgot to confirm and the customer is waiting — what do I do?

Confirm now. The voucher will still generate and SMS-send. If the customer has already left, you can give them the code by phone or in person on their next visit.

What happens if my phone is lost or stolen?

Contact the admin immediately via the support phone or email. They can suspend your account until you regain control of your number. Sign-in is OTP-only so without your phone, no one else can sign in either.

How do I change my mobile number?

Mobile is the primary identifier and cannot be self-changed. Contact the admin to migrate your account to a new number.

Where is my activity history?

Sales shows all confirmed payments. Remit shows all remittances and their approval status. Settings shows your account identity. Both Sales and Remit are exportable as CSV.

Are taxes withheld?

No. As a partner you are responsible for your own tax obligations on the 30% you retain. Speak to your accountant about declaring partner earnings as part of your business income.

How do I close my partner account?

Contact the admin. They will move your account to archived. Outstanding remittance balances remain due even after closure.
