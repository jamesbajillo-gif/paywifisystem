PAYWIFI Partner Privacy Notice

Version 1.0 · Effective 2026-06-03 · Compliant with RA 10173 (Philippine Data Privacy Act)

1. What we collect

  1. Mobile number (primary identifier, mandatory for OTP sign-in)
  2. Full name and email address (mandatory after onboarding wizard)
  3. Store name, optional store address
  4. GCash remittance details (number and account name)
  5. Sign-in IP, device fingerprint hints (for fraud prevention)
  6. Transactional data: confirmed payments, declines, remittances, balance history
  7. Audit log of admin actions involving your account

2. How we use it

  1. Authenticate you and authorize partner actions.
  2. Calculate commission and remittance obligations.
  3. Send transactional SMS (codes, status changes, late-payment alerts).
  4. Compute compliance with weekly remittance rules.
  5. Provide statements, reports, and CSV exports back to you.
  6. Detect and prevent fraud.

3. How we share it

  1. SMS delivery: Semaphore (https://semaphore.co) — for sending OTPs and notifications.
  2. Hosting / tunnel: Cloudflare — for serving the partner portal over HTTPS.
  3. PAYWIFI staff (admin role) — for support and reconciliation.
  4. Government authorities — only when required by Philippine law (e.g. BIR audits, court orders).
  5. We do not sell or rent partner data to third parties.

4. How long we keep it

  1. Active account data: for as long as your account is active.
  2. Transactional records: 10 years (per BIR retention requirements).
  3. Audit logs: 10 years.
  4. OTP codes: 5 minutes (auto-expire).

5. Your rights (RA 10173)

  1. Right to be informed — this notice and any updates.
  2. Right to access — request a copy of your data.
  3. Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data via /partner/settings.
  4. Right to erasure — request deletion, subject to legal retention.
  5. Right to object — opt out of non-transactional communications.
  6. Right to data portability — CSV exports available in Sales.
  7. Right to file a complaint — with the National Privacy Commission (privacy.gov.ph).

6. Security

  1. Sign-in is OTP-only — no passwords are stored.
  2. Sensitive data (Cloudflare tunnel token) is encrypted at rest.
  3. Account lockout after 5 failed OTP attempts.
  4. HTTPS-only access to the partner portal.
  5. Daily SQLite backups retained for 14 days.
