PAYWIFI Partner Terms

Version 1.1 · Effective 2026-06-03

By registering as a PAYWIFI Partner, you agree to operate a cash-payment counter for the PAYWIFI captive portal under the following terms.

Quick summary: You keep 30% of every paid transaction. You remit 70% to PAYWIFI weekly. Miss the weekly deadline and the system automatically restricts your ability to accept new payments until you catch up.

1. Revenue share

  1. Partner commission: 30%. Of every paid customer transaction routed to your partner code, you retain 30% as your earnings.
  2. Remittance obligation: 70%. The remaining 70% of gross collected payments is owed to PAYWIFI.
  3. These percentages are fixed by platform policy and apply uniformly to all partners.

2. Weekly remittance schedule

  1. The week starts Monday 00:00 Asia/Manila and ends Sunday 23:59 Asia/Manila.
  2. You must remit at least 70% of the previous week's gross sales by Monday 23:59 Asia/Manila of the following week (the deadline window).
  3. Remittances are submitted in the partner dashboard with a reference number (GCash, bank transfer, or cash). Admin approval confirms receipt.

3. Payment processing responsibilities

  1. Confirm a payment in the dashboard only after receiving the full cash amount from the customer.
  2. Target service level: confirm within 5 minutes of receiving cash. The system pages you with an SMS if a pending payment passes that mark.
  3. Decline payments for no-show customers, wrong amounts, or anything suspicious, and pick the appropriate reason.
  4. Refunds, if any, are handled by PAYWIFI on a case-by-case basis after admin review.

4. Compliance and automatic restriction

  1. If your previous week's required remittance is not received in full by the deadline, your account is automatically flipped to restricted.
  2. While restricted: you can sign in, view sales and history, and submit a remittance — but you cannot confirm new cash payments and your store is removed from the customer-facing dropdown.
  3. When the outstanding balance is cleared (or an admin grants an override), the system automatically reinstates you.
  4. Repeated delinquency may result in suspension or archival at admin discretion.

5. Statuses

  1. Active — compliant and operational.
  2. Restricted — automatic temporary block due to outstanding weekly remittance.
  3. Suspended — admin-imposed block, typically for breach of these terms.
  4. Archived — partner is no longer active. Historical data is preserved for reconciliation.

6. Account & data privacy

  1. Your mobile number is the primary identifier. Sign-in is OTP-based.
  2. You consent to receive transactional SMS (codes, status changes, late-pending alerts, restriction/reinstatement notices).
  3. PAYWIFI processes the personal data you provide in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012. See the Partner Privacy Notice for details.
  4. You are responsible for keeping the device that receives OTPs secure. Account compromise from a shared device is your liability.

7. Platform usage

  1. You may not transfer, sell, or lease your partner account. Each partner is identified by a unique mobile number.
  2. You may not solicit customers off-platform to bypass remittance.
  3. Reverse-engineering, scraping, or attempting to abuse PAYWIFI APIs is prohibited.

8. Liability

  1. PAYWIFI is not responsible for cash lost, stolen, or miscounted at your counter.
  2. Service is provided as-is. PAYWIFI is not liable for downtime, network outages, or revenue lost during interruptions.

9. Contact

Questions, disputes, or restriction overrides: contact the PAYWIFI admin via the phone or email shown in your partner dashboard footer.
