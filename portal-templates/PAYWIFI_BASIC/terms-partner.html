PAYWIFI Partner Terms

Version 1.0 · Effective 2026-06-03

By registering as a PAYWIFI Partner, you agree to operate a cash-payment counter for the PAYWIFI captive portal under the following terms.

1. Your role

  1. Accept cash from customers buying a PAYWIFI Wi-Fi plan.
  2. Confirm the payment in the partner dashboard so the customer's voucher can be issued.
  3. Remit the gross amount minus your commission to PAYWIFI on the agreed schedule.

2. Commission

  1. Your commission percentage is set by the PAYWIFI admin and visible on your dashboard.
  2. Commission is calculated per paid transaction and accrues to your outstanding balance.
  3. Changes to your commission rate take effect on the date the admin records them and apply to future transactions only.

3. Confirmations

  1. You must confirm a payment only after receiving the full cash amount from the customer.
  2. Target service level: confirm within 5 minutes of receiving cash. Repeated delays may result in suspension.
  3. If a customer pays the wrong amount or does not show up, decline the payment with a reason.

4. Remittance

  1. Submit cash, GCash, or bank-transfer remittances in the dashboard with a reference number.
  2. Cash held more than 7 days unremitted is considered overdue.
  3. The PAYWIFI admin must approve each remittance before it reduces your outstanding balance.

5. Account & data

  1. Your mobile number is the primary identifier and cannot be changed without admin assistance.
  2. You are responsible for keeping the device that receives your sign-in codes secure.
  3. PAYWIFI may send transactional SMS to your mobile (sign-in codes, status changes, late-pending alerts).

6. Suspension & termination

  1. PAYWIFI may suspend or archive a partner account at any time for unpaid balances, fraud, or breach of these terms.
  2. Suspended partners cannot sign in but their historical data is preserved for reconciliation.
  3. You may close your account by contacting the admin. Outstanding balances remain due.

7. Liability

  1. PAYWIFI is not responsible for cash lost, stolen, or miscounted at your counter.
  2. Service is provided as-is; PAYWIFI is not liable for downtime or revenue lost during outages.

8. Contact

Questions or disputes: contact the PAYWIFI admin via the phone or email shown in your partner dashboard footer.
