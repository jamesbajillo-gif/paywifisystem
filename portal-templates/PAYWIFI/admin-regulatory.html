National Privacy Commission — RA 10173

Register as Personal Information Controller (PIC) REQUIRED Organizations processing personal data of 1,000+ data subjects must register with the NPC. As guest count grows this threshold will be crossed. Registration is free.
→ privacy.gov.ph — use the online registration portal.
→ Designate a Data Protection Officer (DPO) — currently: james.bajillo@gmail.com
Designate a Data Protection Officer (DPO) REQUIRED The DPO must be registered with the NPC. The DPO is responsible for ensuring RA 10173 compliance, handling data subject requests, and liaising with the NPC during incidents.
→ Complete DPO registration at privacy.gov.ph after PIC registration.
Conduct a Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) RECOMMENDED A PIA documents what data is collected, why, how it's protected, and what risks exist. NPC recommends PIAs for all systems processing personal data.
→ The audit report (PAYWIFI_Audit_Report_May2026.docx) can serve as the basis for your PIA.

National Telecommunications Commission (NTC)

Verify Permit to Operate requirement VERIFY Commercial public WiFi hotspot operators may require an NTC Permit to Operate as a Value-Added Service (VAS) provider. Single-venue operations may be exempt; multi-location commercial deployments typically require a permit.
→ Contact NTC: ntc.gov.ph or call (02) 926-7722 to confirm your specific requirement.

DTI / SEC Business Registration

Confirm DTI or SEC registration is current REQUIRED Operating a commercial WiFi service requires a valid DTI registration (sole proprietor) or SEC registration (corporation/partnership). Renew annually.
→ dti.gov.ph (sole proprietor) | sec.gov.ph (corporation)

Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR)

Confirm BIR Certificate of Registration is current REQUIRED BIR COR must be posted at your place of business. Voucher sales constitute taxable income. Verify whether VAT (12%) or percentage tax (3%) applies based on annual revenue threshold.
→ bir.gov.ph
Issue official receipts for all paid voucher transactions REQUIRED BIR-registered official receipts (OR) or BIR-accredited e-receipts must be issued for every paid transaction. Currently, PAYWIFI does not auto-generate ORs.
→ Configure the system to email a receipt (or use a BIR-accredited e-receipt provider) for each completed Xendit payment.

Operational Security Reminders

Enable disk encryption on the VM (LUKS) RECOMMENDED Requires a planned maintenance window with VM downtime. Encrypt the Proxmox/VMware VM disk to protect DB and secrets at rest against physical media theft.
→ Reference: PAYWIFI Audit Report S-08. Coordinate with your hypervisor admin.
Configure off-host backup destination (NAS or S3) REQUIRED The backup script is deployed at /usr/local/sbin/paywifi-backup.sh and runs nightly. Uncomment and configure either Option A (rsync to NAS) or Option B (rclone to S3/R2/Backblaze).
→ SSH to server, edit /usr/local/sbin/paywifi-backup.sh and fill in your NAS/S3 credentials.
Store admin credentials in a password manager REQUIRED Document the JWT signing secret location, admin password, and SSH key in a password manager (Bitwarden, 1Password, etc.). The JWT secret is in /etc/paywifi/config.json.
