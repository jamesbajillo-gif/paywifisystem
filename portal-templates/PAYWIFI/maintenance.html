About PAYWIFI
PAYWIFI — The All-in-One Hotspot Monetization Platform
A complete end-to-end solution for hotspot operators, internet cafes, hotels, and any venue offering paid WiFi Access. Handles voucher generation, payment collection, automated SMS delivery — fully automated and cashless, running 24/7.
Features
🎟️
Voucher Management
System-generated codes with configurable length, format, and batch sizes — all managed from one place.
💳
Payment Module
Accept GCash, Maya, QR Ph, cards, and cash — online or over-the-counter.
📱
SMS Module
Automated voucher code delivery via SMS after every confirmed purchase.
🛡️
Secure Admin Portal
Role-protected dashboard with isolated credentials vault — never exposed publicly.
⚡
Instant Delivery
Vouchers generated and sent in real time the moment payment is confirmed.
📊
Sales & Usage Logs
Track sold, used, and expired vouchers across all sources with CSV export.
🌐
Branded Portal
Fully customizable — store name, colors, payment methods, plans, and slides.
🎯
Centralized Management
One dashboard to manage plans, sessions, payments, and devices — fully browser-based, no client software needed.
